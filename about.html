About Us

Kope Cream, established in 2020, is a cafe business that started during the period of pandemic and continues its growing business today. The cafe serves tasty beverages and desserts that are the most popular and unusual favorites of customers. And glady, they are willing to add more to their menu. Rooted in its origin, it was founded by 'ANGELS', which is composed of a group of friends, namely, Helen Faith Santos, Reynalyn Gaylan, Moncy Grace Naron, and Emily Baccay, on January 20, 2020. The cafe started online through Facebook and extended its services on Instagram and TikTok in the first two years. Because of the growing business and requests of most customers, the cafe built its first physical store, located in Pulong Buhangin, Sta. Maria, Bulacan.

Today, the cafe offers online and physical services to its loving customers to satisfy their cravings for the products they serve.
